TECHNOLOGICAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP PROJECT: VENTURE STARTUP COMPANY EZDOC APP
OBJECTIVE

Work in a team to explore basic business theory and concepts through the development of a business plan by using theoretical frameworks of entrepreneurship and an iterative design approach. We are required to create a business model and business strategy for our new technology venture idea. We must research and write a thorough report including BMC, market analysis, marketing strategy, revenue streams and forecast, development plan, operations model, financial model (cost structure and expenses forecast), customer segments, usibility testing, UX design, and design prototypes. My team and I created a hypotheical business venture for an app called EzDoc.

BRIEF

EzDoc was an idea concieved by one of the team members and was further enhanced by the whole team in a collaboration throughout the term course. EzDoc is an online health service booking application developed for the purpose of providing an easy to use self-booking platform to increase patients’ accessibility toward health services in Mississauga. EzDoc aims to offer solutions toward: (1) the long time to access health care and (2) information gap, based on the strength growth opportunity found in the large customer need toward public health services in Ontario. The uniqueness of EzDoc can be presented by its excellent UI design for both patients and clinics to use, company’s partnership with non-profit organization and government department, and the benefits of bringing convenience for temporary residence living in Mississauga.

PROCESS

My team conducted market research and an analysis of business models to complete the report covering the various aspects of a business venture plan. In a hypothetical format, it includes an in-depth review of the company’s SWOT analysis, market and competition, value proposition, solution and differentiation, resource allocation , market lunch plan, and other financial statements and projections, and funding. Finally, we developed the basic front-end interface of the app in an iterative approach through UX/UI principles.
