OVERVIEW

Youth Gravity is a Toronto-based, youth-led, not for profit organization aimed towards providing a platform to young leaders to pursue projects that they are passionate about. Our mission is to ensure that the youth within our community are empowered and that they have equal access to leadership, community engagement and community development opportunities that reflect the needs of our local youth.

Youth Gravity is the first youth-led team in Canadian history to receive the Community Resilience Fund from the Government of Canada- Ministry of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness to fund Impact the Outcome. This project aims to equip youth with the language they need to have conversations around social polarization, hate-motivated violence and radicalization.

Rebranding

For my first self-directed initiative as Marketing Director of Youth Gravity, I proposed, designed, and developed a complete rebranding for Youth Gravity as I believed that the old logo and branding needed updating for a effective look. The previous logo was unusable due to file formatting errors from my predecessor, where it could not be converted or transferred. Additonally, The brand theme did not reflect Youth Gravity's mission. In order to build our reputation and gain awareness for our cause among youth in Toronto, it was necessary to rebuild the branding.
