ONLINE ADVERTISING AND MARKETING: MARKETING CAMPAIGN PROPOSAL FOR ANUSUAL
OBJECTIVE

Work in a team to develop an online advertising and marketing campaign proposal for an adopted client. Use project management tools to complete the project deliverables collaboratively. We must run the campaign on behalf of the client and compare analytics to determine the success of our strategy. Our chosen client for this project was ANusual Drapery and Decor.

SKILLS & CONCEPTS: Marketplace Evaluation, SWOT Analysis, Porter's Forces, PESTEL Analysis, Digital Marketing Strategy, KPI's, Ad Design, Data Analysis, Financial Budgeting, Research

BRIEF

This online marketing and advertising plan was prepared for ANusual Drapery and Decor. Established in 2011, ANusual Drapery and Decor’s goal was to address the homeowner’s need for covering non-standard windows. Since then the business has grown to include custom made textile decor and has serviced interior designers, home stagers and realtors. In the Spring of 2019, ANusual plans to release a new service for custom and rental wedding backdrops and drapery.

As ANusual did not have a functional marketing plan nor a brand identity, my team had to start re-branding the company from scratch. To redesign the image for this company to create brand diffrentiation and improve ROI was identified as one of the deliverables along with developing a clear online marketing strategy. This proposal improved brand awareness by up to 65% according to our final results. By boosting website traffic through re-branding, positioning, and marketing ANusual to the a client base through a re-launch, my team allowed ANusual to become a competetive player in the wedding industry.

RESULT

After conducting formal research for the business strategy from market research, consumer analysis, and assessment of business models to complete the professional report for the company's evaluation, my team outlined the re-launch of Anusual. We were able to generate a defined social media strategy with mockeups and templates, advertising budget, online ad campaign using SEO tools, developed the brand identity, strategy for ongoing optimization using A/B testing, and design the static webpage.
