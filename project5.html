DIGITAL COMMUNICATIONS: AWARENESS CAMPAIGN LOOKUP
OBJECTIVE

Design a prototype for an original educational awareness campaign on behalf of a hypothetical company that targets a specific social problem using elements of persuasive appeal, elements of classical rhetoric, community consultations, and scholary research. Finally, dicuss the persuasive intent, the projected audience, conduct a visual analysis and consider the ethical responsibilities, benchmarks and potential challenges.

TOOLS USED: Adobe Illustrator
SKILLS & CONCEPTS: Communication, Research, Visual Analysis, Brand Strategy, Graphic Design, Illustration

BRIEF

LOOKUP is a non-profit organization that I proposed to advocate for the mental and physical implications of children using digital devices. The awareness campaign that I developed for this issue is titled "The Electronic Pacifier" which aims to bring credible and scientific information regarding the harmful effects of mobile devices to parents of 1-3 year old children. This campaign involves the use of a traditional advertising using billboards and posters to enourage parents to follow the organization's directions to look up and gain important knowledge rather than to their screen devices which perpetuate a toxic cycle of mental and health issues. The prototype for the advertisment as well as the organization logo was created from scratch following a literature review of scholarly research and studies in the field to allow me to develop a persuasive campaign that is both appealing and informative.

THESIS STATEMENT: The usage of electronic devices is scientifically proven to have detrimental consequences on children aged 1-3, including

  • Brain and learning ability
  • Body and health
  • Social interactions

RATIONALE & RESULT

Below is the final prototype for "The Electronic Pacifier" that I designed using Adobe Illustrator. The campaign draws from preliminary research in order to appeal to the logos, ethos, and pathos of rhetorical persuasion as well as visual semiotics to address the cause effectively. The Electronic Pacifier represents the parenting trends within todays technologically advanced society in an infographic format. The campaign draws on the metaphor of a smartphone being used the same way a pacifier would to generate a conversation on modern parenting. The image provides a juxtaposition where the silhouette of a baby sucks on a pacifier that is connected to a smartphone. The child's brain is made to look like it is mechanical to represent the transformation they endure psychologically, changing their chemical balances.

The intention of this awareness campaign is to:

Motivate

  • The goal is to motivate parents to create a healthy balance of screen time and play time to keep children safe.

Encourage

  • The hope is to encourage parents to make the right decision with the knowledge they gained.
