HAND PAINTED RETRO STREETWEAR
BRIEF

This is a self-directed UX/UI project that I worked on for my brand concept. HASiD is a fashion brand that aims to combine 90’s urban streetwear and retro HipHop fashion with the identity of diaspora from the streets of Toronto. The ethnic diversity of the city is seen within the its local vocabulary that draws from language references of generations of immigrants who make up a lot of the population. Hasid (حاسد envious) is a word rooted in Arabic, existing within Somali and Urdu, that has integrated into mainstream slang in areas of Toronto. Focusing on the nostalgia and sentiment associated with the words that exists in Toronto slang, HASiD recontextualizes the multilingual identity of diaspora through urban streetwear that is inspired by the 90’s HipHop genre. HASiD adopts graffiti prints and fonts in colorful hand painted patterns to emphasize the Arabic/Urdu word which juxtaposes the Latin text, representative of retro urban style.

ROLE: UX/UI Designer
TOOLS USED: Adobe XD & Illustrator
SKILLS & CONCEPTS: User Research, User Flow, Ideation, Brand Design, UX/UI Design

OBJECTIVE

HASiD offers a variety of options for clothing, including customization. A mobile app for the brand will eliminate issues that come with placing orders online by providing an interactive platform where customers can access collections or exclusive product, choose from various style options, customize color pallets, as well as receive customer service. HASiD will enter the e-commerce for fashion marketplace as a mobile application to provide a platform that allows customers to browse through, customize, and purchase hand painted clothing while providing customer service.

DEFINING THE USER

To understand the backgrounds of the potential app users and determine their needs, three distinct personas can be identified. The target audiences that the app will serve are youth and young adults (ages 15-30) who fit into one of 3 groups:

  • Fashion enthusiasts who wish to customize the clothing that HASiD offers to fit their needs
  • Those who want to shop the collection and have access to latest releases
  • Customers who require support or wish to track their purchases

PRODUCT IDEATION

With a defined set of targeted users and their core motivations and passions, the user flow can be developed. As the first step in the design process, user flow considers the user research into the design. The app should be easy to use to fulfil the purpose of the target users and brand intention. HI-FIDELITY WIREFRAMES

The following is an overview of some of the key screens in the hi-fidelity mockup of the app.

SHOP

This is the "Shop" screen where users can browse available products. This page allows access to the collections and the customization service. The static navigation at the bottom remains consistent on every page to quick access to the main functions of the app.

COLLECTIONS

The "Collections" screen is one of the most fundemental pages of the app as an e-commerce platform. From the "Shop" screen, users are redirected to the products and can scroll through the latest collection, displayed at the top of the screen, browse through previous collections or original products. This screen is decorated with colorful banners that represent the brand theme and model the products. The products are featured in a two column format and displays the price of each item.

PRODUCT

Once a user selects a product from the collection screen, this single product view page appears for a full view of the item along with details for specs. Users can select their preferred size or quantity and add the item to the cart through a modern button selection.

CUSTOMIZE

The "Customize" feature is crucial for the apps goal and company's focus. The customize screen allows users to design their own HASiD t-shirt by modifying color pallets and mix n' match. The switch on the left hand side allows customers to switch the base color of the product while the colors at the bottom modify the overlay. Customers can choose to select either a top or bottom, select a size, and any choice of color pallete from the HASiD themes.

PROFILE

The "Profile" page can be accessed from the navigation menu at the bottom of the screen to review and edit log-in information, mailing address, and payment methods. The bottom portion of the profile page is dedicated to providing customers with a compiled purchase history.

INFORMATION

This is the "Information" screen which fulfils one of the user purposes and brand mission to provide customer support. It is essential for businesses to offer personal and direct customers care for retention and loyalty. This page features an embedded feedback and support form beneath a short mission statement.
